## About Sage Haus

Sage Haus helps busy families build and hire the support systems they deserve. Founded by Kelly—a mom of three—Sage Haus was born from her own experience of how finding a house manager changed her life.

We're dedicated to helping parents experience that same relief through:

- **🏠 Hiring Services** — Find the perfect house manager fit for your family
- **📋 Home Systems** — Frameworks to onboard and implement practical home systems

## Our Mission

Modern families carry more than they ever stop to acknowledge—the logistics, the planning, the mental load. We believe a house manager isn't a luxury; it's the missing piece of infrastructure that helps families thrive.
